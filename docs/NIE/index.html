Nature-Inspired Engineering

Exploring, deciphering and conceptualizing the mechanisms in biological systems that have been shaped by billions of years of evolution

We translate this understanding into the design of bio-inspired surfaces that can dynamically tune interfacial and transport properties, such as wettability, adhesion, thermal-fluid transport, for applications in water-energy nexus and biomedical applications.

Research Areas

Our interdisciplinary approach combines physics, chemistry, and engineering to create innovative solutions inspired by nature

Surface & Interface

Investigating interfacial phenomena and surface modifications to control wettability and adhesion properties.

Thermal-fluid

Studying heat and mass transfer in biological and artificial systems for energy-efficient applications.

Droplets & Bubbles

Analyzing the dynamics of droplets and bubbles on various surfaces for microfluidic and heat transfer applications.

Microfluidics

Developing microfluidic devices and systems for precise fluid control and biomedical applications.
